The Market Failure We're Solving

Core Engineering Philosophy

The Three Laws of Production-Grade AI

01

Architecture Over Intelligence

Seven capable agents, operating without coordination, produce outcomes worse than three mediocre agents that are well-coordinated. Raw model capability is the last bottleneck. System design is the first.

→ MCP/A2A orchestration layers

02

Compliance is Code

"The model said so" is not a legal defense. Compliance must be baked into middleware via Open Policy Agent (OPA) rules and immutable ClickHouse audit trails — not bolted on as an afterthought when the regulator calls.

→ OPA policy-as-code enforcement

03

Zero Trust Agents

Agents should be treated like contractors with limited access badges, not employees with master keys. Every tool call is scoped, every action is reversible, and every privilege is explicitly granted — never assumed.

→ Least-privilege tool scoping
