The ANO Operational Stack

What our engineers actively monitor and manage, 24 hours a day.

SLO / SLA Management

Uptime tracking, latency percentiles (P50/P95/P99), error rate trending, and escalation thresholds by agent criticality tier.

Cost Engineering

Token budget management, semantic cache tuning, intelligent model routing (premium vs. economy models by task type), runaway loop detection.

Chaos Engineering

Quarterly GameDays simulate agent failures, context injection attacks, LLM outages, and knowledge staleness events to validate system resilience.

Compliance Watchdog

Continuous OPA policy enforcement monitoring, audit log verification, and regulatory change alerts that trigger automated impact assessments.

Conflict Resolution

Active monitoring of agent coordination failures, arbitration rule tuning, and post-mortem analysis for recurring conflict patterns.

Knowledge Ops

Automated freshness score monitoring, scheduled re-ingestion pipelines, and stale-document quarantine with human review queues.
